President's Welcome

Welcome to the website of the Caribbean Centre for Educational Planning. This website is designed to provide you with information and data on educational issues pertinent to your country and other countries of the Caribbean. The site also provides space for interactive engagements among educational planners and practitioners. These interactive engagements are expected to support you in your work whether you are a graduate student, a policy maker or adviser, a head of an educational institution, or a classroom teacher. This site can also be used to make requests of the CCEP for help in sourcing information as well as placing orders for services.

Services offered by the CCEP

As you will see from the “Services Button”, the CCEP offers a range of services to support the educational planning and policy needs of countries, institutions, and educational practitioners. You are invited to make use of those services. We also have the capability to offer customized responses to your organization’s needs and our prices are affordable. We want to ensure that this site serves your needs, so we welcome your feedback. We are committed to our mission of supporting educational planning in Caribbean and Latin American countries with the end that they acquire sustainable capacities in educational planning, resulting in a higher quality of life for citizens. In pursuing our goal of supporting the acquisition of sustainable capacities in educational planning we regard you as a partner and therefore invite you to become a member of the CCEP.

Canute S. Thompson

PhD; CMC Head, CCEP
